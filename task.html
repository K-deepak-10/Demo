Projects

Chatbot Development

Legal Chatbot for Indian Legal Reforms I developed a legal chatbot utilizing Llama-2 and Mistral, fine-tuned with data from the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, and Bharatiya Sakshya Bill of 2023. The chatbot provides accurate, insightful, and empathetic responses to legal queries. Using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), it ensures up-to-date guidance on criminal offenses, procedural changes, and evidence standards. This project highlights the potential of AI in revolutionizing legal assistance by delivering reliable information in real-time.
